Plan integracii API "Oplata chastinami" dlya saita MyAdmin

1. Chto uzhe est v proekte
- Na saite uzhe est scenarii onlain-oplaty cherez LiqPay.
- V zakaze sposob oplaty hranitsya cherez PaymentMethodEnum.
- Checkout uzhe umeet:
  - sozdat zakaz,
  - perevesti na stranicu oplaty,
  - prinyat callback,
  - otmetit zakaz oplachennym,
  - otpravit uvedomleniya i fiskalizirovat.

2. Rekomenduemaya arhitektura
- Ne vstraivat "Oplatu chastinami" priamo v CheckoutController cherez uslovnye if mono.
- Sdelat novyi shluz, naprimer:
  - MonoInstallmentsService
  - MonoInstallmentsController
  - novyi enum sposoba oplaty, naprimer MONO_PARTS
- Eshe luchshe srazu vvesti obshii kontrakt:
  - PaymentGatewayInterface
  - realizacii: LiqPayGateway, MonoInstallmentsGateway

3. Predlagaemyi user flow
1) Polzovatel na checkout vybiraet "Oplata chastinami".
2) Zakaz sozdaiotsya v statuse "ozhidaet oplaty".
3) Servis Mono sozdaet payment/session/application cherez ih API.
4) Polzovatel redirektitsya na stranicu oplaty Mono ili na lokalnyi route tipa /checkout/{order}/pay/mono-parts.
5) Mono posylaet webhook/callback.
6) My:
   - validiruem podpis,
   - pishem log,
   - obnovliaem status zakaza,
   - sohraniaem vneshnii payment_id/application_id,
   - zapuskaem te zhe post-payment processy, chto i dlya LiqPay.

4. Chto nuzhno dobavit v kod
- Novyi sposob oplaty v PaymentMethodEnum.
- Novyi servis: app/Services/MonoInstallmentsService.php
- Novyi controller: app/Http/Controllers/Front/MonoInstallmentsController.php
- Novye routes dlya:
  - start/redirect na oplatu,
  - webhook,
  - pri neobhodimosti return/success.
- Novyi config: config/mono_installments.php
  - base_url
  - merchant_id
  - secret
  - webhook_secret
  - sandbox/prod flag

5. Hranenie dannyh i logi
- Dobavit tablicu logov po analogii s liqpay_logs, naprimer mono_payment_logs.
- Predpochtitelno hranit:
  - provider
  - provider_payment_id
  - provider_status
  - redirect_url
  - payload
  - confirmed_at
- Esli u "oplaty chastinami" est statusy created / approved / rejected / canceled, ih luchshe hranit otdelno ot samogo zakaza.

6. Integraciya v tekuschii checkout
- V CheckoutController:
  - dobavit novyi payment_method v validaciyu,
  - zamappit ego v novyi enum,
  - posle sozdaniya zakaza otpravliat ne v LiqPay, a v route dlya mono parts.
- V admin / order view zhelatelno pokazyvat:
  - tip rassrochki,
  - vneshnii status Mono,
  - vneshnii ID zaiavki/platezha.

7. Chto vazhno produmat
- Idempotentnost webhook:
  povtornyi callback ne dolzhen duplirovat pisma i fiskalizaciyu.
- Sverka summy:
  summa iz callback dolzhna sovpadat s order->grand_total.
- Srok zhizni platezhnoi sessii.
- Otkaz / timeout / cancel / reject scenarii.
- Vozmozhnost povtorno otkryt ssylku oplaty dlya zakaza.
- Polnye logi dlya podderzhki.
- Razdelenie sandbox i production.

8. UX rekomendacii
- Na checkout pokazat:
  - "Oplata chastinami Monobank"
  - dostupnye sroki 3 / 6 / 10 i t.p., esli eto podderzhivaetsya API.
- Pered sozdaniem zakaza sohranyat:
  - chislo platezhei,
  - tip programmy.

9. Prakticheskii plan vnedreniya
1) Podtverdit tochnyi Mono-produkt i sandbox credentials.
2) Opisat state machine statusov.
3) Sdelat novyi payment method v checkout.
4) Realizovat service + controller + webhook.
5) Dobavit payment logs.
6) Privyazat post-payment obrabotku kak u LiqPay.
7) Protestirovat sandbox scenarii:
   - success
   - reject
   - duplicate webhook
   - timeout
   - povtornaya popytka oplaty

10. Rekomendaciya po etapam
- Etap 1: bystro sdelat otdelnyi shluz MonoInstallments po analogii s LiqPay.
- Etap 2: potom vynesti LiqPay i Mono v edinyi payment abstraction layer.

11. Klyuchevoi vopros pered startom
- Nuzhna imenno bankovskaya "Oplata chastinami Monobank", ili obychnaya online-oplata Mono, gde rassrochka predlagaetsya na storone banka?
- Eto nado utochnit do nachala razrabotki, potomu chto arhitektura, polya formy i callback flow mogut silno otlichatsya.
